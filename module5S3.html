Sustainable Courses


The Mascaro Center for Sustainable Innovation (MSCI) curated a list of sustainable courses offered by Pitt schools. Check out a few classes here:
  • Global Environmental Politics (PS 1542): Explore the political side of environmental issues. How do we address environmental problems in the global arena?
  • Gender and Sustainability (GSWS 1450): Approach sustainability with a focus on gender and intersectional identities. How does gender play into creating a more sustainable future?
  • Current Issues in Sustainability (ENGR 1905): Apply the core principles of sustainability to a variety of relevant issues. How do we approach current environmental problems?
  • Sustainability and Computing (CS 0090): Analyze the impacts of tech giants such as Amazon and the intersection of computers and the environment. How do we mitigate the ecological impact of computers?
